About the Space Shack

Welcome to the Space Shack, located on the newly discovered planet Phaedrus-1711. This planet is known for its vast, glowing deserts, stunning view of a nearby nebula, and its mysterious ancient ruins. Guests of the Space Shack will enjoy luxury accommodations in the heart of this unexplored frontier.

Our goal is to provide a unique and unforgettable experience while discovering the beauty and mystery of Phaedrus-1711. Whether you're here for a relaxing stay or to embark on an intergalactic adventure, the Space Shack is the perfect base for exploring the unknown.
