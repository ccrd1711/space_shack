About the Space Shack

Welcome to Phaedrus-1711. Welcome to Space Shack. This is the future.

Phaedrus-1711, discovered in 2023, is a newly discovered planet in the Andromeda Galaxy. This planet is known for its mountainous terrain, fabulous views of its ringed brother planet Kerouac-47 (and further away sister planet Ishmaela-1992 on a bright day!), and of course the Dark Matter Lake.

Since its discovery, Space Shack has been the premier destination for intergalactic travelers seeking a unique and tranquil getaway. Katy Perry to the edge of the atmosphere? Old news. Come and experience the wonders of Space Shack, where you can relax in our luxurious, sought-after accommodation, explore the breathtaking landscapes, and enjoy the serenity of space.

If you need any more proof, check out our guest reviews to see what others have to say about their stay at Space Shack.
